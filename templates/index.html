How it works

Paper version

Each person writes 4-6 characters/people on a strip of paper, then puts them in a shared hat. You then take turns to take a name from the hat. Round 1 you can use any words to describe the character but the word itself. If someone guesses it then they keep get the point. Put all the paper back in the hat at the end of the round.
