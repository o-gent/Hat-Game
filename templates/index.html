How it works

You'll need 4-10 people and you favourite video calling platform (zoom sucks ü§Æ) to call those people while you play.

Each person writes 2-6 characters/people/beings to put into the hat, these should probably be funny or interesting in some way. A random item from the hat is chosen each time a new player takes a turn, the round ends when the hat runs out of items. Round 1 you can use any words to describe the thing you have been given from the hat, apart from the words you are given. The player who guesses the item gets a point. Round 2, all the items are restored to the hat but this time you can only describe it with one word. Round 3 you're only allowed to act out the item!
